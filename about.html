Life:

My name is Stephanie White and I am from a small farming town located in South Florida called Belle Glade. My hometown is mostly known for the football athletes it produces but it is also known for being a key 'player' in the sugar industry.

Education:

I am a fourth-year undergraduate student at the University of Florida majoring in Telecommunications and minoring in African American Studies.

Why I chose telecommunications:

I love people! Perfecting how to properly communicate with others is highly valuable in the journalism and communications world. It is extremely hard to have a successful career without networking and establishing great business relationships.

After graduation:

I have not decided on what subject I would like to teach or where I would prefer to be located. If my application is denied by TFA then I will take a year off before starting graduate school in Business Administration to volunteer with a non-profit organization, preferably American Red Cross.
